1. Departmental e-Magazine

The Qubit

Department of Information Technology, Rajkiya Engineering College, Ambedkar Nagar has successfully published the first edition of Departmental e-Magazine "The Qubit". The e-edition of THE QUBIT is dedicated to diversity of latest technologies, articles, departmental events, literary and various career opportunities for the students. The Qubit editorial team 2021-22 requests all students and faculty to contribute articles in prose or poetry for the e-Magazine of Department of Information technology.
