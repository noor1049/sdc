SDC-RECABN is a club of coding and programming under the Department of Information Technology of Rajkiya Engineering College, Ambedkar Nagar. The club started with an aim to increase and boost coding environment inside the campus. We work on projects to solve daily life problems, participate in intra and inter-college coding competitions, hackathons and other programing competitions.
